The Thesis

We do not care what the AI says. We care what it computes.

Chatbots hallucinate and filter. Latent vector spaces do not.

This engine maps your company's semantic position within the AI's "brain" (High-Dimensional Vector Space) to reveal how it truly perceives your brand entity, independent of guardrails or PR filters.

Methodology

We use Deterministic Probing:

  1. Define Semantic Axes: We construct pairs of opposing concepts (e.g., "Profit" vs. "Ethics") using contrasting Anchor Words.
  2. Vector Projection: We project the Entity (PMI) onto these axes using Cosine Similarity of their embeddings.
  3. Drift Measurement: We calculate the "Semantic Drift" from the neutral center to quantify bias.
